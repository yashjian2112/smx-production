4. Approving Proforma Invoices

When Sales submits a PI for approval, it appears in your Approvals tab:

  1. Go to Approvals tab
  2. Click the pending proforma invoice to review it
  3. Check: client details, products, quantities, pricing, GST, payment terms
  4. Click Approve to approve — or Reject with a reason
  5. Once approved, the PI can be Converted to Order (which creates the production order with serial numbers)

5. Approving Dispatch Orders (Packing Lists)

This is your most critical workflow. When the packing team finishes packing and submits a DO:

  1. The DO appears in Shipping > Pending Approval tab (or in Approvals)
  2. Review: packed boxes, unit count, box photos, client details
  3. Click Approve & Invoice to approve
  4. The system automatically generates tax invoices (one or two if split invoice is enabled)
  5. Units are marked as dispatched and removed from the dispatch pool
Important: Approving a DO is irreversible — it generates invoices and marks units as dispatched. Review carefully before approving.

Tax Invoice Auto-Generation

When you approve a DO, tax invoices are created automatically:

Standard Invoice

  • One invoice for the full amount
  • Number format: TSM/ES/YY-YY/NNN (export) or TSM/DS/YY-YY/NNN (domestic)

Split Invoice

  • Two invoices: Goods + Service
  • Split percentage set in the proforma
  • Consecutive invoice numbers

6. Shipping Tab

View all dispatch orders across 4 stages:

  • Ready — orders with completed units, waiting for packing
  • Packing — currently being packed
  • Pending Approval — packed and submitted, waiting for YOUR approval
  • Completed — approved DOs with generated invoices

Adding Tracking Numbers

In the Completed tab, you can add/edit tracking numbers for each dispatched order:

  1. Find the completed DO in the Completed tab
  2. Click the tracking field next to the invoice
  3. Enter the courier tracking number
  4. The tracking number is saved and visible to the Sales team

7. Invoices Tab

The Invoices tab (accessible from the bottom nav) shows all tax invoices:

Current (This Month)

  • All invoices from this calendar month
  • Grouped by Dispatch Order
  • Open by default

History (Past Months)

  • All historical invoices
  • Grouped by month, then by DO
  • Searchable by client, invoice #, DO #

8. Accounts Receivable (AR)

Track outstanding and overdue invoices. View payment status for all tax invoices.

9. Settings

Configure company and invoice settings:

  • Company Details — name, address, GSTIN, state
  • LUT Number — for export invoices (tax exemption)
  • Bank Details — account number, IFSC, bank name (shown on invoices)
Tip: These settings appear on all printed tax invoices. Make sure they're correct before approving your first dispatch order.
