PURCHASE MANAGER

1. Overview

As the Purchase Manager, you manage the complete procurement lifecycle — from requirement orders generated by the store, through RFQs and vendor quotes, to purchase orders and goods receipt. You ensure materials are available for production at the right time and best price.

4. The Procurement Flow

Materials procurement follows this lifecycle:

Requirement Order→
RFQ→
Vendor Quotes→
Purchase Order→
GAN→
GRN

Step 1: Requirement Orders (RO)

ROs are generated automatically when:

  • Job cards are dispatched — when materials are issued to employees, if stock is low, an RO is created
  • Low stock alert — when a material falls below minimum stock level
  • Manual creation — you or the store manager can create custom ROs

Review pending ROs and approve them to proceed to the RFQ stage.

Step 2: Request for Quotation (RFQ)

  1. Select approved ROs to bundle into an RFQ
  2. Choose which vendors to invite for quotes
  3. Set a deadline for vendor responses
  4. Send the RFQ — vendors receive it via the Vendor Portal
  5. Optionally request samples from vendors before committing

Step 3: Evaluate Vendor Quotes

Once vendors submit their quotes:

  • Compare pricing across vendors side by side
  • Review price breakdowns (if configured by Admin)
  • Check vendor performance ratings from past orders
  • Select the best quote

Step 4: Purchase Order (PO)

  1. Convert the selected vendor quote into a PO
  2. PO number is auto-generated (format: TSM/PO/YY-YY/NNN)
  3. Review items, quantities, and pricing
  4. Send PO to vendor

Step 5: Goods Arrival Note (GAN)

When the vendor notifies that goods are arriving:

  • Create a GAN from the PO
  • Specify expected arrival date
  • When goods arrive at warehouse, the Store Manager verifies and creates a GRN

Step 6: Goods Receipt Note (GRN)

After the Store Manager completes the GRN, stock levels are automatically updated. You can then track payment status for the PO.

5. Vendor Management

  • View and manage all vendor records
  • Track vendor performance ratings (delivery time, quality, pricing)
  • Manage vendor categories
  • Enable vendor portal access for self-service quoting
Tip: Vendor performance scores are calculated automatically based on past PO fulfilment. Use these scores when selecting vendors for new RFQs.
